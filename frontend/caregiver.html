About the Caregiver Dashboard

As a caregiver (family member, spouse, or helper), you can:

  • View linked patients — See everyone who has added your email as their caregiver.
  • See medication lists — Check what medicines each patient is taking, dosages, and schedule times.
  • Get missed-dose alerts — When a patient marks a dose as missed, you receive an alert (email or in-app) so you can follow up.
  • Support adherence — Use the adherence summary to see how well they’re following their schedule and when they might need a reminder or help.

Patients must add your email when they register (or in their account) and choose you as their caregiver to link you.

🔔 Reminders for you (Caregiver)

You get reminders when: A linked patient marks a dose as missed. The app sends you an alert with the patient’s name, the medicine, and the scheduled time so you can check in or remind them.

Use the button below to load your linked patients and their current medications. Check this page regularly to see their status and any missed doses.
