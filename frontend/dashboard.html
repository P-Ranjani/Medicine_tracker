About this app

Medication Tracker helps you take your medicines on time and keeps a record of your adherence. You can:

  • Scan prescriptions — Upload a photo of your prescription to extract medicine names and dosages.
  • Build your schedule — Add medications with times and dates; the app supports multiple medicines at once.
  • Track adherence — Mark each dose as taken, missed, or skipped and see your adherence percentage.
  • Check interactions — See potential drug interactions (informational only; always ask your doctor).
  • Ask the AI chatbot — Get simple answers about what your medicines do and side effects.
  • Stay connected — Link a caregiver (e.g. family) so they can view your medications and get alerts if you miss a dose.

🔔 Reminders for you

How reminders work: The app uses your medication schedule to remind you when it’s time to take your medicine. Reminders can be sent as browser notifications, email, or in-app alerts so you don’t miss a dose.

Make sure you’ve added your medications and set the correct times in Medications. Then record each dose in Adherence so your caregiver can see how you’re doing and you can track your progress.

Tip: Allow notifications in your browser to get reminder alerts.
